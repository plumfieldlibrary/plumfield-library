Welcome to Plumfield Living Library!

Welcome to Plumfield Living Books Library in Denmark, Wisconsin! We are a small private lending library with about almost 10,000 books at this time. You can find us on Facebook and Instagram.

Also, you can email us here: plumfieldlivinglibrary@gmail.com.

We are proud partners with Biblioguides. All our library patrons have a Biblioguides membership included in their annual fee. You can find our Partner Guide information here: Plumfield Guide

In addition to the books themselves, our library has a lot of enrichment opportunities. We have monthly book clubs, a nature club, a handicrafts club, and a monthly Symposium.

How does this work?

This is a private lending library open to members.

How much does this cost?

$100 per year per family plus any class or club material fees.

What is included in my membership?

  • Full Access to the library (over 10,000 books)
  • A one-year membership to the amazing Biblioguides database and community (a $79 value)
  • Access to in-person book clubs at the library (for middle and high school students; possibly moms too)
  • Opportunity to connect with other families through the library and its activities
  • Access to Nature, Handicrafts, Creative Writing and Symposium Classes and Clubs
  • A subscription to our book review newsletter, Book Review Roundup
